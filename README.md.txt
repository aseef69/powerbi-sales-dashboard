📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)
📌 Business Problem

Businesses generate large amounts of sales data, but it can be difficult to quickly identify top-performing products, profitable regions, and sales trends.

The goal of this project is to build a Power BI dashboard that helps stakeholders monitor sales performance, analyze profitability, and identify growth opportunities.

🎯 Project Objectives

This dashboard was designed to:

Track key business KPIs

Identify high-performing product categories

Analyze sales performance by region

Monitor monthly sales and profit trends

Identify loss-making products

Support data-driven decision making

📊 Key Business Insights

From the analysis, several important insights emerge:

1️⃣ Technology Dominates Revenue

Technology products generate the highest revenue among all categories.

2️⃣ Consumer Segment Drives Sales

The Consumer segment contributes the largest share of total sales.

3️⃣ End-of-Year Sales Growth

Sales and profit increase significantly during Q4, indicating strong seasonal demand.

4️⃣ Loss-Making Subcategories

Some product sub-categories such as Bookcases and Binders generate negative profit, suggesting pricing or cost issues.

5️⃣ Sales Growth Over Time

Sales performance improved from 2019 to 2020, indicating overall business expansion.
